## Analytical Framework

When reviewing the CIM, apply the following analytical lenses:

### Business Model Assessment
1. **Revenue Model**: How does the company generate revenue? Recurring vs. non-recurring? Contract-based vs. transactional?
2. **Value Proposition**: What is the competitive advantage? Is it sustainable?
3. **Customer Base**: Concentration risk? Retention rates? Contract terms?
4. **Market Position**: Market share, growth trajectory, competitive dynamics
5. **Scalability**: Unit economics, operating leverage, capacity constraints

### Financial Profile Assessment
1. **Growth Trajectory**: Historical revenue and EBITDA growth rates
2. **Margin Profile**: Gross, EBITDA, and net margins vs. industry benchmarks
3. **Cash Conversion**: EBITDA-to-cash-flow conversion efficiency
4. **Capital Intensity**: CapEx requirements as % of revenue
5. **Balance Sheet Strength**: Leverage ratios, working capital efficiency

### Red Flag Indicators
Watch for and flag any of the following:
- Revenue growth significantly outpacing industry
- Margins materially above or below peers without clear explanation
- Large or unusual related party transactions
- Aggressive accounting policies (revenue recognition, capitalization)
- Significant customer or supplier concentration
- Pending or threatened litigation
- Environmental or regulatory exposure
- Key person dependency
- Recent changes in auditors or accounting policies
- Significant off-balance-sheet arrangements
