- [ ] Every client feedback item is addressed with a clear response and action

- [ ] Mathematical calculations are verified
- [ ] Terminology is used consistently throughout

- [ ] Escalation items are identified for partner review
- [ ] The report maintains professional objectivity (not influenced by client pressure)
- [ ] No references to any external tools or platforms
