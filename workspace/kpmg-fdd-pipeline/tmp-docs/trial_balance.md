## Notes to Trial Balance

1. **Cash Accounts:** Total cash of $7,418K comprised of operating ($4,218K), payroll ($812K), money market ($2,000K), and CAD operating ($388K converted at year-end rate of CAD/USD 0.73).

2. **Accounts Receivable:** Gross AR of $5,314K less allowance of $155K (2.9% of gross). Unbilled AR of $245K relates to implementation projects in progress at year-end.

3. **Capitalized Software:** Gross capitalized development costs of $5,986K with accumulated amortization of $2,546K. Net balance of $3,440K amortized over 3-year useful life. FY2025 additions of $1,800K.

4. **Goodwill:** $1,200K from acquisition of ScheduleRight (a small scheduling IP purchase) in Q3 2021. No impairment identified.

5. **Deferred Revenue:** Total current deferred revenue of $8,603K comprised of subscriptions ($7,843K), implementation ($486K), and support ($274K). Substantially all to be recognized within 12 months.

6. **Term Loan:** $3,000K outstanding under SVB term loan facility. Original principal $5,000K (Oct 2022), 5-year term, quarterly principal payments of $125K plus interest at SOFR + 3.50%. Current portion of $500K classified under GL 2500.

7. **Stock-Based Compensation:** $280K of SBC expense related to options granted under the 2018 Equity Incentive Plan. Allocated across departments: $42K COGS, $56K S&M, $112K R&D, $70K G&A. Included in respective salary line items above for simplicity; shown separately in GL 9400 for EBITDA add-back purposes.

8. **Intercompany:** Toronto operations (Test Corp Canada ULC) are consolidated. Intercompany management fees and cost allocations totaling $2.1M are eliminated in consolidation. Transfer pricing follows arm's-length principles per the June 2024 transfer pricing study.

9. **§174 Capitalization:** Under IRC §174, the Company is required to capitalize and amortize R&D expenditures (5-year domestic, 15-year foreign). Deferred tax liability of $3,045K reflects cumulative §174 capitalization impact.
