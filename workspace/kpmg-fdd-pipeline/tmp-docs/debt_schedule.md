## 7. Change of Control Provisions

The SVB Credit Agreement contains a change of control provision requiring:

1. **Mandatory Prepayment:** All outstanding amounts under the term loan and revolver become immediately due and payable upon a change of control event.
2. **Change of Control Definition:** Acquisition of 50% or more of outstanding voting stock, or sale of all or substantially all assets.
3. **Notice:** 30 days' prior written notice to SVB.

The proposed acquisition by Apex Growth Partners would trigger the change of control provision. Management anticipates that the term loan balance (~$3,000K at close) will be repaid from transaction proceeds or refinanced under a new credit facility arranged by Apex Growth Partners.
