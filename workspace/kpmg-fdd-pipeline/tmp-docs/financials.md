## 5. Revenue Recognition Policy

The Company recognizes revenue in accordance with ASC 606 (Revenue from Contracts with Customers):

- **Subscription Revenue:** Recognized ratably over the subscription term (typically 12 months) beginning on the date the customer is given access to the platform. Contracts are generally non-cancellable with annual billing.
- **Implementation Revenue:** Recognized over the implementation period (typically 30–90 days) using a percentage-of-completion method based on labor hours incurred.
- **Support Revenue:** Recognized ratably over the support term, which is coterminous with the subscription period.

Deferred revenue represents amounts billed in advance of revenue recognition, primarily annual subscription billings. As of December 31, 2025E, the Company expects $8.6M of deferred revenue, substantially all of which will be recognized within 12 months.
